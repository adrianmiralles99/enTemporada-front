¿Por qué comer
alimentos de temporada?


Es fácil encontrar todo tipo de frutas y verduras en cualquier momento del año. Pero, para elegir las opciones más sostenibles, es recomendable comer productos locales y de temporada. Estas son las razones a tener en cuenta durante tu próxima compra.

Mejor sabor
y más fresco

By respecting its natural cycle, you get rewarded with fruit and vegetables that are packed with flavor. An apple that was freshly picked from the field yesterday will taste much better than one that was picked last week and imported from far away. Not only do they taste better, fresher food lasts longer too.

Menor impacto
ambiental

There’s a bigger carbon footprint behind your groceries if it travelled half the world before it ends up in your fridge. Seasonal fruit and vegetables that come from a field near you contribute to the sustainability of the food system by reducing energy consumption and generating less carbon dioxide emissions.

Apoya a tu
comunidad local

By buying food grown in your area,  you are contributing to local businesses and supporting your community. Since your fresh food moves through fewer steps, more of the money you spend tends to get to the people growing it. To make the biggest local economic impact, seek for producers who pay their workers a fair wage and practice social justice in their business.
